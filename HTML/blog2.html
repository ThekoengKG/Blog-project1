The Great Outdoors – Make Your Party an Alfresco Affair

Host a picturesque party on the green at Buckhurst Park (www.buckhurstpark.co.uk) in Hartfield, West Sussex. Famous for being the home of Winne the Pooh’s ‘Hundred Acre Wood’, the historic estate has 2,000 acres of parkland, including a lake and formal garden. The impressive setting is ideal for team building activities such as rounders, archery and treasure hunts, and the catering team can put together a picnic to be proud of – just make sure honey sandwiches are on the menu!

Messin’ about on the water is a fantastic way to spend a summer’s day and Lagoon Watersports @ Brighton Marina (www.lagoon.co.uk/brighton-marina-watersports-centre) offers a city-based coastal location. Team-building or client-based events can be catered from 30 to 400 people, and guests can learn new skills and have fun with challenges such as raft building, stand up paddle boarding and kayak water polo. Adrenalin fans can also book sailing tuition, powerboating trips and jet ski hire. When the sun goes down, check in to Malmaison Brighton (www.malmaison.com) a boutique hotel right on the waterfront, and enjoy cocktails on the decked terrace.
