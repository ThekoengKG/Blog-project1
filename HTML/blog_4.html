A Christmas Affair - Modern & Vintage Holiday Party

by Carolina

Posted on January 22, 2021 at 15:00 PM

I am so excited to share this party with you today! Last week, I shared my version of a kid’s Gingerbread house decorating party, but we all know Moms love to decorate too! Last December, I hosted a ladies Christmas party for my friends. We had hot cocoa and treats, talked about the Christmases of our childhoods, and decorated our gingerbread houses and cookies. It was a perfect Mother’s Morning Out!

This party is extra special to me, not only because I got to celebrate with my lady friends, but because it was featured as the cover story for Mingle Magazine! Yes! Pinch me please!! I hosted the party at one of my favorite venues in the Austin area, One Eleven East. It was the perfect location for this party, because I wanted to give this celebration a mix of modern and vintage flair.

To bring my vision of this party to life, I used suitcases, cookie cutters, and toys that had the retro look I was going for. The vintage items came from a lovely little shop, called The Nest Box.
