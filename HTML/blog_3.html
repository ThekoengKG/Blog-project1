The Best Tropical Birthday Party Ideas for Kids

by Carolina

Posted on January 1, 2021 at 12:00 PM

Summer is here - which means it’s time to celebrate summer parties at the pool or with anything that involves water. And, of course tropical vibes are always a top favorite for summer parties including birthdays. So, when my client’s daughter was turning 10, I gave her the best tropical birthday party in her own backyard.

Come along as I take you through the entire tropical birthday party which was designed with lots of palm leaves, pineapples and flamingos for the biggest tropical feel. Tropical colors were chosen in hot pink, lime green, aqua blue, orange and white. Key areas that I designed included the ever popular dessert table, the photo booth which is always a kid favorite, the drink station and food table. Other design areas included the popsicle stand, welcome and gift tables, party favors, adult drink area and the tropical decorations throughout the backyard.
